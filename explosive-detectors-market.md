<p><p>The explosive detectors market features several key types, each utilizing unique technologies. X-ray explosive detectors employ radiation to visualize concealed explosive materials in baggage and cargo. MRI explosive detectors use magnetic resonance imaging for detailed internal inspections, suitable for critical security applications. Steam explosive detectors sense changes in pressure and temperature caused by explosives, while neutron explosive detectors analyze neutron emissions to detect specific isotopes related to explosives. Each type offers distinct advantages in security and detection applications.</p></p>
