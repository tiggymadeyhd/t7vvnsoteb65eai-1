<p><strong>The Food Irradiation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Radiation</li><li>Gamma Radiation</li><li>X-Ray Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food irradiation market comprises three main types: electron beam radiation, gamma radiation, and X-ray radiation. Electron beam radiation uses high-energy electrons to sterilize food, effectively reducing pathogens while preserving quality. Gamma radiation employs radioactive isotopes, like cobalt-60, for deep penetration and is widely used for bulk products. X-ray radiation combines the benefits of both methods, delivering high energy for effective treatment with minimal heat. Each technology aims to enhance food safety and extend shelf life, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
