<p><p>The mango seed oil and butter market features two primary extraction methods: cold-pressed and expeller-pressed. Cold-pressed extraction involves mechanically pressing the seeds without heat, preserving the oil's natural nutrients and flavor, making it ideal for cosmetic and culinary uses. In contrast, expeller-pressed extraction uses heat and pressure, which can enhance yield but may alter the oil's properties. Both types cater to diverse applications in skincare, food, and wellness, appealing to consumers seeking natural products with varying qualities and benefits.</p></p>
